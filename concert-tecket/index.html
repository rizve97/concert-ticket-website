Shohid Shorone - Concert for Martyrs

About The Event

A fundraising concert organized by students in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. All proceeds from the concert will go directly to the families of the students in Bangladesh who passed away during the 2024 quota reform protests and Anti discriminatory movement. There is no political association of this event or the organizers. By the students, for the students.
Bangladeshi artists from all over the USA are coming out to Atlanta in support of the students in Bangladesh and their movement, to perform at this event. None of the artists are accepting any artist fees for their efforts or performance. All proceeds and donations will go directly to Bangladeshi families affected during the protests.

Behind ventXout

ventXout is formed by 2 students in Atlanta, Georgia, USA, who came together for their student counterparts in Bangladesh. The two students, Aqeeb Iqbal and Mahir Ishraq, met doing music for the Bangladesh Student Association at their university. Later, they formed ventXout to organize this concert, in efforts of helping the families affected by the 2024 quota reform protests and anti discriminatory movement in Bangladesh.
